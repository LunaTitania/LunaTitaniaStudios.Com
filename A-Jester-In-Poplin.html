A Jester In Poplin

Adam Van de Groot is a cheerful, extraverted 13 year old boy living in the quiet suburb of Brighton,Michigan and to celebrate finishing the eighth grade, goes on a ghost hunt with some friends in the local haunted house, but gets far more than he bargained for! Aw well, at least it won't be a boring summer vacation!
